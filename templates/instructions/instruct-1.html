Instructions

In the experiment, there are 125 rounds. On each round you will see a set of blue and yellow dots. On each round, you will decide whether that set contains more blue or more yellow dots.

Each set is independent from the others — just because there were more blue dots on one round does not mean there will be more blue dots on the next round.

Depending on how many correct answers you give, you can earn a bonus of up to $1.00. The first 5 rounds are practice and will not affect your bonus.
