Instructions

On other rounds, you will not get to see the dots yourself; instead, you will be told what other participants in a previous batch decided when they got to see the dots. The decisions you will see are genuinely those of other workers on MTurk who played before you.

If you don't get to see the dots for yourself, one of three things will happen:

1. You will see what one person in the previous batch thought:

Someone in the previous batch decided:
BLUE

2. You will see what everyone in the previous batch thought:

In the previous batch:
12 people decided BLUE and 28 people decided YELLOW

3. You will see what everyone in the previous three batches thought:

Three batches ago:
25 people decided BLUE and 15 people decided YELLOW

Two batches ago:
28 people decided BLUE and 12 people decided YELLOW

One batch ago:
6 people decided BLUE and 34 people decided YELLOW

Note that each batch has 40 participants, so if you are told what a previous batch decided, the total number of people deciding blue and yellow will always sum up to 40.
